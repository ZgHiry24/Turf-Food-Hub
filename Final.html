About Us

How Turf Began Turf was founded on December 10th 2020 in the middle of the pandemic, and during the times when it was all very uncertain in the world, and all the people wanted a little comfort, a spot of safety, and also something nice to drink. In those days restaurant out was strictly on the sub, and many people were looking for a nice, open space where they could relax, breathe, and have good food and drinks, so that’s when we decided to start this Turf not only as a business, but as a community club. We did the “test drive” and started just serving them smoothies, milk teas, halo-halo, lemonade and soda pops really nice things to do that were cool and colorful and would touch people’s hearts. And over time, Turf stopped just being a place for food it became a place for families, friends, neighbors even in masks and at safe distances from each other. We’ve always tried to cram as much life into every cup and serving we give and today Turf continues to grow with that same philosophy of comfort, community and cooling drinks.
